By clicking "I Agree," you provide explicit permission for Wirokit Oy to process your CV and share it with potential hiring clients as described below.

1. Identity of the Data Controller

Wirokit Oy is the "Data Controller" for the personal data you provide. You can contact our Data Protection lead at tuomo.tuominen@wirokit.com for any privacy-related queries.

2. Purpose and Legal Basis

We process your CV data (name, contact info, employment history) to match you with potential career opportunities. The legal basis for this processing is your explicit consent (Article 6.1.a of the GDPR).

3. Recipients of Your Data

By agreeing, you authorize us to share the generated CV with our third-party clients (categories include: hiring managers, HR departments, and department heads within the IT sector). Your contact information will not be shared with any third parties. We will not sell your data to anyone.

4. Retention Period

In accordance with the principle of storage limitation, your data will be stored until the end of your employment contract with Wirokit Oy or up to 14 days if no such contract exists. After this period, your CV and all associated personal records will be permanently and securely deleted from our database.

5. Your Statutory Rights

Under the GDPR, you have the following rights:
  • Right to Withdraw: You can withdraw your consent at any time.
  • Right of Access & Rectification: You can request a copy of your data or ask us to correct inaccuracies.
  • Right to Erasure: You can request that we delete your data before the retention period ends.
  • Right to Complain: You have the right to lodge a complaint with a Data Protection Authority (Office of the Data Protection Ombudsman in Finland).
    • All data queries should be sent to tuomo.tuominen@wirokit.com.
